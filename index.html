welcome

At Rising Caf we are passionate about serving quality home-baked goods, deliciously innovative classic dishes and excellent beverages in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. We aim to provide a wonderful place to relax, socialise, work, hold meetings and, above all, enjoy exceptional-quality food and drinks at an unbeatable prices. Run by previously homeless and substance-addicted men and women, 100% of proceeds go directly towards funding their recovery, and that of many others around the country, through the charity work of Betel UK.
